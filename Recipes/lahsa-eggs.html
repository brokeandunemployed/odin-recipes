Lahsa Yemeni eggs

discribtion

Lahsa a delicious yemeni dish recipe, its a mixture of onions,tomatoes and cheese mixed with spices and herbs.

ingredients for lahsa

  • eggs
  • onions
  • green pepper
  • black pepper
  • cumin
  • salt
  • tomatoes
  • cream cheese

steps

  1. Cut 1 large onion and two large tomatoes
  2. cook onions until they start to yellow on a pan with olive oil
  3. add the cut tomatoes, and start seasoning while sterring with salt and pepper and cayan
  4. add eggs and stir until cooked.
